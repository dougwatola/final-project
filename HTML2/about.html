about HAR (Human Activity Recognition)

  2. HAR is a field that studies how the body moves and how technology can detect those movements in various ways. It encompasses everything from recording when and how fast you are walking to learning how tallented people assemble complex machines. The research possiblities are enless and are only limited by your imagination.

Accelerometer

Normally it's what tells your phone how to orient the screen properly, but it's capable of so much more...it is capable of measuring the change in velocity.

Gyroscope

Adds another dimension of movement tracking in your phone especially rotation or twist. Very sensitive part of your device and can pick up phone and air vibrations.

Magnetometer

It's in both smartphones AND tablets. This sensor is what detects your divices position in relation to Earth's magnetic north. Super cool!
